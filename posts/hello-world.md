This is the first post sourced from the [neilk17/public-writing](https://github.com/neilk17/public-writing) GitHub repository.

I wanted to share more of my writing in public but there was too much friction in sharing it on other platforms. I tried using Substack and Bearblog but I always wanted to create a place where I owned the stack. Maybe in the future I will share the same writing on other platforms like Bearblog, Substack and X.

The goal is to do more long form writing about the things that I'm interested in. Which are currently AI, programming, manufacturing, physics. I also want to share book notes from the books that I'm reading.
